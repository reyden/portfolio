It serves as the first step in the procurement process. This system is designed to formally document, track, and manage requests for purchasing goods or services before they are approved and procured.

Projected Benefits of the project.

With customizable features, role-based permissions, and secure data management, the system promotes transparency, reduces unauthorized spending, and supports better organizational planning and resource allocation.
